Tom's Morning Routine
Tomning ertalabki odatlari

Tom wakes up at 7 o'clock every morning. He gets up quickly and goes to the bathroom. First, he brushes his teeth and washes his face.

After that, Tom has breakfast. He never skips breakfast.

At 8:15, he leaves the house and takes the bus to work. He always arrives at the office on time.

Grammar · Grammatika
G
Present Simple
Hozirgi oddiy zamon
Formula
Qoida
Subject (Ega) + Verb (Fe'l) He / She / It → fe'l + -s / -es
When do we use it?
Qachon ishlatamiz?
  • Regular habits & daily routines Muntazam odatlar va kundalik tartib
  • Facts that are always true Har doim haqiqat bo'lgan faktlar
  • Schedules & timetables Jadvallar va vaqt jadvallari
Examples from the text · Matndagi misollar
1
Tom wakes up at 7 o'clock every morning.
Tom har kuni ertalab soat 7 da uyg'onadi.
2

U hech qachon nonushtani o'tkazib yubormaydi.
3
She goes to work by bus.
U ishga avtobus bilan boradi.

💡
Remember: Add -s / -es only when the subject is he, she or it. I / You / We / They use the base form.
Eslab qoling: -s / -es faqat he, she, it bilan ishlatiladi. I / You / We / They bilan fe'lning asosiy shakli ishlatiladi.
