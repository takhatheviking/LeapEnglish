Introductions Framework
Tanishish uchun shablon
Hello + I'm / My name is ...  |  This is ...  |  Nice to meet you  |  How are you?
Steps to introduce yourself · Tanishish bosqichlari
1. Salom bering: Hello / Hi
2. Ismingizni ayting: I'm Tom / My name is Tom
3. Boshqasining ismini so'rang: What's your name?
4. Tanishuvni yakunlang: Nice to meet you
5. Hol so'rang: How are you?
Examples from the lesson · Darsdan misollar

Useful responses & variations · Foydali javoblar va variantlar
+ Hi, I'm Umar. / Hello, my name's Amir. (ikkala variant ham to'g'ri)
+ Pleased to meet you. (Nice to meet you ning rasmiy shakli)
+ I'm fine, thanks. / Very well, thanks. / Not bad.
? Where are you from? / Qayerdansiz?
? What do you do? / Nima bilan shug'ullanasiz?
💡
Tanishayotganda kulib qo'l bering. Ingliz tilida madaniy odat shu. "Nice to meet you" deganda ohangni yuqoriroq ko'taring, bu iliq va do'stona chiqadi. Va eng muhimi: javob bering, jim qolmang. "Fine, thanks" yoki "I'm good" yetarli.
