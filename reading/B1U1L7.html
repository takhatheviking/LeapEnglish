Greetings by Time of Day
Kun davomida salomlashish
Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Night + "Good ..."  |  e.g. Good morning!
When to use · Qachon ishlatiladi
Good morning: ertalabdan to peshingacha (taxminan 12:00 gacha)
Good afternoon: peshindan to kechgacha (12:00 dan 18:00 gacha)
Good evening: kechki payt uchrashganda (18:00 dan keyin)
Goodnight: faqat yotishdan oldin yoki tunda xayrlashganda
Examples from the lesson · Darsdan misollar
Good morning! What a lovely day!
Xayrli tong! Juda chiroyli kun!
Good afternoon! A coffee, please.
Xayrli kun! Bitta kofe, iltimos.
Good evening, everyone!
Xayrli kech, hammaga!
Goodnight! Sleep well.
Xayrli tun! Yaxshi uxlang.
What a lovely day!
Qanday chiroyli kun!
