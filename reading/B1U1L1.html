Grammar · Grammatika
📘
Verb 'to be': am / is
'To be' fe'li: am / is
I + am  |  He / She / It / This + is  |  e.g. I am Tom. / This is Serena.
When to use · Qachon ishlatiladi
To say who you are / O'zingizni tanishtirganda
To introduce other people or things / Boshqa odam yoki narsani tanishtirganda
Examples from the lesson · Darsdan misollar
