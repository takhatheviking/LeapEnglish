Plurals: One and Many

I have one book. You have five books. Tom has three phones.

In the street I see two buses, four bikes, and six houses.

For lunch, Tom has two sandwiches and three apples. Serena has one watch, but Tom has two watches.

Grammar · Grammatika
📘
Plurals: -s / -es
Ko'plik shakli: -s / -es
book + s = books  |  bus + es = buses  |  watch + es = watches
When to use · Qachon ishlatiladi
Ko'pchilik so'zlarga -s qo'shiladi: book / books, phone / phones
-ch, -sh, -s, -x bilan tugasa, -es qo'shiladi: watch / watches, bus / buses
-o bilan tugaganlarga odatda -es: potato / potatoes, tomato / tomatoes
Talaffuz 3 xil: /s/ books, /z/ phones, /iz/ watches
Examples from the lesson · Darsdan misollar
You have five books.
Sizda beshta kitob bor.
I see two buses.
Men ikkita avtobus ko'ryapman.
Tom has two sandwiches.

Serenada bitta qo'l soati bor.
Tom has two watches.
Tomda ikkita qo'l soati bor.
Negative & Question forms · Inkor va so'roq shakllari
− I don't have three books, I have one. / Menda 3 ta kitob yo'q, 1 ta bor.
− These aren't my watches. / Bu qo'l soatlari meniki emas.
? How many books do you have? / Sizda nechta kitob bor?
? Are these your phones? / Bu telefonlar sizningmi?
