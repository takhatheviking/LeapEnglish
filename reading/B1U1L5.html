Vocabulary · Lug'at
📘
Numbers 1 to 10
1 dan 10 gacha sonlar
1 one / 2 two / 3 three / 4 four / 5 five / 6 six / 7 seven / 8 eight / 9 nine / 10 ten
Key points · Muhim nuqtalar
"two" so'zida w harfi o'qilmaydi (tu, tw emas)
"eight" so'zida gh harflari o'qilmaydi (eyt)
"three" dagi th ingliz tilida alohida tovush (tri emas, sri taxminan)
Sonlar har doim otdan oldin keladi: two books, five pens
Examples from the lesson · Darsdan misollar
I have two books.
Menda ikkita kitob bor.
My bus is number five.
Mening avtobusim 5-raqamli.
I have three pens in my bag.
Sumkamda uchta ruchka bor.
My phone number has nine numbers.
Telefon raqamimda to'qqizta son bor.
Let's count from one to ten.
Birdan o'ngacha sanaymiz.
Negative & Question forms · Inkor va so'roq shakllari
− I don't have ten books, I have two. / Menda 10 ta kitob yo'q, 2 ta bor.
− My bus isn't number six. / Mening avtobusim 6-raqamli emas.
? What's your phone number? / Telefon raqamingiz nima?
