What's this in English? It's a book. And this? It's a phone.

Grammar · Grammatika
📘
Articles: a / an
Aniqlovchi artikllar: a / an
a + undosh (b, k, p, t, s...)  |  an + unli (a, e, i, o, u)  |  e.g. a book / an apple
When to use · Qachon ishlatiladi
a: so'z undosh tovush bilan boshlansa (a book, a phone, a laptop)
an: so'z unli tovush bilan boshlansa (an apple, an umbrella, an orange)
Birlikdagi otdan oldin doim a yoki an bo'lishi shart
Muhim: tovush muhim, harf emas (an hour, a university)
